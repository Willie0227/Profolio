Where Am I Study At Now?


I am currently a High school freshman student, studying in Taiwan, Hsinchu County. The school I am in is the Pacific American School. I transferred to the Pacific American School in 2019 and stated the education there to prepare myself to attend college out of Taiwan.



I like the education style in the Pacific American School because it provides me an environment with English teaching educations and also professional teachers experts on their major.



In my high school education, I also attend different sports clubs for after school activities, such as frisbee and badminton. These activities help me release my pressure on my homework load. More importantly, I believe that educations need to include some entertainment elements in order to let a person feel comfortable, instead of always focusing on reading, writing, and mathematics.



In the future, I plan for my further education carrier to at least a master's degree. I want to focus on the major in Computer Science, which is one of my major hobbies. My dream is to become an engineer, hence, I take classes related to computer science, such as Java, C++, and APCS in the future.
